House Shows

House shows feature a rotating line-up of pro stand-up comics who perform at clubs and colleges around the city. Each show usually lasts about an hour to an hour and a half.

No cover, 1 drink minimum. Enjoy $5 margaritas and half-off wine all night.

Show times:

Sunday-Thursday: 8pm, 10pm

Friday-Saturday: 6pm, 8pm, 10pm, Midnight

Attendance is limited, so please use the form provided to reserve seating.

About The Lantern

The Lantern Bar & Restaurant, located on historic Bleecker Street between Sullivan & Thompson, is an old-fashioned Greenwich Village joint harkening one back to the famous Black & Tan days of Bleecker Street.
